Our Platform

Three Pillars.
Real Initiatives.

These aren’t talking points. Every initiative below is a concrete action we’re ready to push from day one in office.

01
CULTURE

Building a campus life worth showing up for — events that bring Eagles together and memories worth making.

🏊 Late Night Swim
Keep our great pool space open late one weekend a semester — snacks, games, music, and a great time.
🧠 Mental Health Day
Once a semester, one day off from classes so students can de-stress and enjoy fun campus activities.
🎢 Disney Day
Once a semester, SGA shuttles ride free of charge, driving any student with a ticket to the Disney Parks.
02
INFORMED

You pay your SGA fee — you deserve to know where it goes and have a real voice in how this university is run.

📊 Monthly Budget Updates
Every month, students receive an SGA email breaking down our budget for that month. No more guessing.
🏛 Town Halls
Public meetings where YOU can talk directly to school leaders, express concerns, and propose solutions in person.
📋 Union Tabled Office Hours
We bring the conversation to YOU — office hours held in high-traffic public areas, not tucked away in the SLS office.
03
EVERYDAY SGA

SGA shouldn’t disappear after election season. We’re committed to being visible, useful, and accessible every day.

🚌 Weekend Shuttle Routes
A new weekend route running to Target and Publix — because every student deserves access to basic essentials.
🚗 Campus Zip Car
Partner with Zip Car to let any student rent a car from campus — freedom of movement without owning a car.
💡 Lights & Cameras
More lights in dark campus spots and cameras near high-theft areas to protect students’ bikes, skateboards, and gear.
